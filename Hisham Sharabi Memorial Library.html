Hisham Sharabi Memorial Library

Diabetes is affecting Palestinians in disproportionate numbers.

The Hisham Sharabi Memorial Library at The Jerusalem Fund, one of the largest Palestine-centered collections in the United States, is a specialized resource center dedicated to the study of Palestine and the Arab world. Named after Dr. Hisham Sharabi, a prominent Palestinian intellectual and founder of The Jerusalem Fund, the library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, and multimedia materials. It serves as a vital resource for scholars, researchers, and community members, fostering a deeper understanding of the region’s history, culture, and political issues. The library also hosts events and provides a quiet space for study and exploration

The book collections are non-circulating and intended primarily for independent reading and research within the Fund, books cannot be taken outside the Fund. If you wish to use the library, our doors are open from Monday to Friday, between 9:30 am and 4:30 pm

Through educational events, humanitarian support, and cultural preservation, the Jerusalem Fund is helping build the movement for Palestinian liberation
