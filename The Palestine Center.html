Education | The Palestine Center

The Palestine Center is an independent educational program committed to communicating reliable and objective information about the Palestinian political experience to American policy makers, journalists, students, and the general public.

Annual Conference

Our annual conferences bring together policymakers, academics, and activists to promote a deeper understanding of the issues facing Palestine, the Middle East, and the United States’ relation to the region.
