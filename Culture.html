Culture

Our gallery showcases contemporary art by Arab-American and Arab artists, specializing in the work of Palestinian artists and art centering on issues of the Arab and Islamic world.

Exhibition Inquiries

Gallery Al-Quds welcomes inquiries from artists wishing to exhibit in our space. Please send an email with a short resume and several digital samples of your work to Duna Dabbas (duna@thejerusalemfund.org). Gallery Al-Quds reserves the right to review all work for appropriateness.
